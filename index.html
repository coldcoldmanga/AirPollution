What is AQI

The Air Quality Index (AQI) is a measurement of air pollutant concentrations in ambient air pollution and their associated health risks.

Healthy 0-50
Air Quality is considered satisfactory, and air pollution posses little or no risk.
Moderate 51-100
Sensitive individuals should avoid outdoor activity as they may experience respitory symptoms.
Unhealthy for Sensitive Group 101-150
General public and sensitive individuals in particular are at risk to experience irritation and respitory problems.
Unhealthy 151-200
Increased likehood of adverse effects and aggravation to the heart and lungs among general public.
Very Unhealthy 201-300
General public will be noticeably affected. Sensitive groups should restrict outdoor activities.
Harzadous 300+
General public at high risk of experiencing strong irritations and advere health effects. Should avoid outdoor activities.
